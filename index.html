Welcome to Free Sound Object Mixer (FSOM).

FSOM is an open-source object based audio engine written in C++.

Authors and Contributors

FSOM was written by David Moore (@drm004e), David Devaney (@ddevaney) and Stephen Pearse (@spearse) at the University of Sheffield Sound Studios.
